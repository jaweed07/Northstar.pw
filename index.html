The Northstar Story

Northstar was founded with a single goal:

To bridge the glaring gap between strategic consulting and on-ground execution for MSMEs. Our founders, having worked across top corporates and grassroots startups, saw firsthand how small businesses struggled not for lack of ambition, but for lack of structured guidance, market insight, and hands-on support.

Our Mission

Making Elite Consulting Accessible to All:

At Northstar, we believe every business-big or small deserves world class, results driven consulting once reserved for Fortune 500s.

We were founded with a bold purpose:

We bridge the gap between talent scarcity and untapped expertise through a distributed consulting model-delivering affordable, expert-led, and outcome driven solutions.

Our mission is simple yet transformative:

To help businesses everywhere scale faster, operate smarter, and reach their own Northstar sustainable success.
